About

  • Artist Statement

    I utilize poetry and design to connect surrealist imagery to every day life. I explore this for both myself and my audience to better understand the world we inhabit, and I work closely with familial relationships, specifically mother/daughter. I also work with young love, and the self discovery that comes via ones relationship to other people. I believe that human connection is the key to better understanding that around us.

  • Bio

    My name is Rachel Justice, and I am a Connecticut based poet and designer who attends the half-day program at the Greater Hartford Academy of the Arts. I have been published in Connecticut Student Witers Magazine in 2017, and a 2018 Fresh Voices Winner where I performed alongside Andrea Gibson at the Sunken Garden Poetry Festival. My eye for visuals and knack for words makes me a multi-facteted artist able to communicate understanding through multiple platforms

To Andrew Reardon, who helped me make this site and showed me Enfield kids can be more than what meets the eye. To every Art and English teacher I had in Elementary to Highshool. Thank You.
